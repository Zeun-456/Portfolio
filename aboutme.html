About Me

Hi! I'm Ellie Song, a game developer based in Australia. I have a burning passion for game design and development , and I love creating immersive experiences that bring joy to players. My journey in game development started with a fascination for game playing, particularly Nintendo games (it sounds cliche but Mario games were my favourite), and it has grown into a full-fledged dream where I can combine my creativity with technical skills. I am also a skilled artist, thus I enjoy adding my own creativity to creating and designing levels/maps, characters, cover arts and more. I hope to fulfill my dream of becoming a game designer and developer that can reach out to many people and make them happy through my games.
